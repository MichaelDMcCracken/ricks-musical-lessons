Meet Your Instructor.


Affectionately known as "Pastor Rick", Rick Cobb has been teaching private music lessons for over 30 years in the Ormond Beach Conservatory of Music. Pastor Rick served faithfully for 35 years as Pastor of Music and Worship at Riverbend Community Church in Ormond Beach, Florida. Pastor Rick received his Bachelor of Music Education degree from Florida State University (Tallahassee, FL). He later received his Master of Music degree from Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary (Ft. Worth, TX).


Rick has been married to his wife, Claudia, for 42 years. Rick and Claudia were homeschool parents to their six children.
